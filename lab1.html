About Me

Hi, I am Precious Aghaeze, I am a Masters of design student at UC Berkeley. A Product Designer + Engineer passionate about integrating design with code.

Selected Projects

Project 1: Immersive Sky Umbrella

A weather-responsive umbrella that lights up the canopy with fiber optics and projection, letting users create their own personal sky.

Project 2: emBO – Empathy as a Boundary Object

A speculative design project that uses audio-visual feedback to mediate conflict and support empathy. emBO serves as a third party in tense conversations, making emotions visible without judgment.

Project 3: Experience Airdrop (Xdrop)

A speculative wearable necklace that captures and shares emotionally rich sensory memories between loved ones across distance.
